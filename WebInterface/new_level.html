Level Creator Syntax

Example: !,CR,LHLL3,GND,BS,GND,CR,LHLR3,GND,BS

  • ! - Spawn Point
  • CL - Crouch Left
  • CM - Crouch Middle
  • CR - Crouch Right
  • JL - Jump Left
  • JM - Jump Middle
  • JR - Jump Right
  • LHLLX - Left Leg, Left Side, X seconds
  • LHLMX - Left Leg, Middle, X seconds
  • LHLRX - Left Leg, Right Side, X seconds
  • LHRLX - Right Leg, Left Side, X seconds
  • LHRMX - Right Leg, Middle, X seconds
  • LHRRX - Right Leg, Right Side, X seconds
